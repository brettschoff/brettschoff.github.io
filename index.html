Who am I?

I am a team-oriented software engineer who has curiosity and passion for working with technology. My background is in the automotive industry as parts salesman and bodyshop estimator. During that time I developed my customer service skills and also honed my skills for collaborating as a team to overcome challenges. I want to bring my passion and dedication as a developer to help develop something that will help ease or bring joy the daily life of everyone on the planet.



I love to learn how things work. I take apart equipment to see what makes it tick. I look at code just to see how it was made. Once I learn how something is made I try to think of ways I can recreate it. Either in a more efficent way or a more creative way. This is where I get my aspiration to be able to create something that will bring joy or ease to the lives of many people accross the globe.
